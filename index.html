Welcome to Smart Integration

Smart Integration is a platform for all the immigrants and Finnish people who would like to socialize, exchange cultural values and get to know each other better in a comfortable environment. Immigrants who are working class, jobless, housewives or even retired personals who wants to know the culture and values of their new home country are welcomed with open hearts. We do not expect anyone to know Finnish or English perfectly to be with us. Finns who would like to welcome their new neighbours from different countries, get to know them and make friends with them are welcomed warmly. We do many indoor and outdoor activities to keep it exciting and motivating for all of our visitors. Inspirational stories of fellow immigrants, failures, mistakes and their success are shared at every event. This is a good opportunity to meet these people personally and ask tips and suggestions about your own problems and struggles.  We at smart integration do not discriminate anyone of their gender, race or religion. We invite all of you who would like to try a new way of integration to the Finnish society.
